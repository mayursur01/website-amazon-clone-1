About Amazon

Overview Mission Services

Overview

Amazon, founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, is one of the largest e-commerce platforms in the world. It started as an online bookstore and has since expanded to offer a wide range of products and services, including cloud computing, streaming, and artificial intelligence.

Mission

Amazon’s mission is to be Earth’s most customer-centric company, where people can find and discover anything they want to buy online. The company strives to offer low prices, vast selection, and fast delivery.

Services

  • E-commerce: A vast marketplace for products ranging from electronics to groceries.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): A leader in cloud computing services.
  • Prime Video: A popular streaming platform for movies and TV shows.
  • Alexa: An AI-powered virtual assistant.
  • Kindle: A leading e-reader brand.
